Data ContributionÔÉÅ

The NMRlipids Databank is open for contributions from the community. Current data deposited at NMRlipids/BilayerData is shared under CC-BY 4.0 License.

You can add new simulation data, experimental data, or update existing entries. Addition of new molecules is also very wellcome. It is also possible to make your own data repository and plug it into the NMRlipids Databank instead of BilayerData.
